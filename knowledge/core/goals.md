# knowledge/core/goals.md
# Last updated: May 2026

## What Stormrake is
Elite Digital asset brokerage. Education, custody, and trade execution
through direct broker relationships. Not an exchange, not a platform.
Clients call a broker — a highly-skilled human answers.

## Differentiator
The broker handles everything: educates, executes, and supports
custody decisions. No chatbot. No ticket queue. One relationship.

## Custody
Clients choose self-custody or Stormrake custody. Both are supported.
Stormrake custody uses segregated institutional-grade cold storage —
assets are not commingled or rehypothecated. We recommend our custody
product; we do not require it.

## Markets
- Australia: AUSTRAC-registered, direct operations
- United States: operated through Zero Hash (they provide platform
  and regulation; Stormrake provides the client service layer)

## Goals
- Be the most trusted digital asset brokerage in AU and US
- Deliver elite client experience on every interaction
- Stay ahead of market events — clients never need to look elsewhere
- Grow without diluting service quality

## Account types
Individual, corporate, SMSF, trust, ATF.

## Security and privacy
Security is a top priority, not a feature. Clients trust us with
significant assets and personal information — that trust is never taken
for granted.

- Client data is private. Never referenced, shared, or exposed beyond
  what the client has explicitly authorised.
- Clients should never feel anxious about their assets or information.
  Our job is to handle everything so they do not have to.
- Security posture is always on. Not reactive — proactive.

## Hard constraints
- General advice only — never personal financial advice
- Never treat any client as routine or low priority
- Never comingle or rehypothecate client assets
- No unverifiable claims: "best", "guaranteed", "optimal" are prohibited
- Never describe as exchange, platform, or fintech

## Key facts
- Track record: 7+ years continuous operation, zero client losses
- Services: education, custody (client choice), trade execution
- Language: "digital assets" not "cryptocurrency" — "client" not "customer"
