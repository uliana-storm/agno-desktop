Spot Bitcoin ETFs See Largest Single-Day Outflow Since January

The 11 U.S.-listed spot Bitcoin exchange-traded funds have recorded over $1.5 billion in cumulative net outflows since May 7. On Monday, the largest single-day outflow of $649 million occurred — the biggest since January this year.

Market Dynamics

Seller Dominance Across Spot and Derivatives Markets

  • Cumulative Volume Delta (CVD) — Spot Market: Shifted from positive $16.9 million to negative $126.2 million, indicating sustained seller pressure through market orders.
  • CVD — Perpetual Futures: Dropped to negative $368.5 million, confirming aggressive short positioning in derivatives markets.
  • Options Hedging Activity: Put option premiums have risen and delta skew increased from 10.9% to 14.4%, suggesting options traders are pricing in greater downside risk.
